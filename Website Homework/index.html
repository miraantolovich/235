Wingspan

Wingspan is a game that focuses on being a birdwatcher.

You are bird enthusiasts—researchers, bird watchers, ornithologists, and collectors—seeking to discover and attract the best birds to your network of wildlife preserves. Each bird extends a chain of powerful combinations in one of your habitats (actions). These habitats focus on several key aspects of growth:
  • Gain food tokens via custom dice in a birdfeeder dice tower
    • >
  • Lay eggs using egg miniatures in a variety of colors
    • >
  • Draw from hundreds of unique bird cards and play them
The winner is the player with the most points after 4 rounds. If you enjoy Terraforming Mars and Gizmos, we think this game will take flight at your table.
